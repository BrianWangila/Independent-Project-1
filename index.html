Stories So many memories.

Orientation day

The orientation day at Moringa school is motst important first day you can ever hope for. You are taken through all the rules while at the school, the do's and dont's and what is expected of you to achieve success and become a top-notch software developer. Don't even think about missing this important/special day for the start for a long and productive journer.

Board meeting

The board meeting is another most important space at Moringa School. It is where great minds meet to talk about the future of students. Students also meet up to present their ideas and offer others vital insights on web development. The space is well-lit and ample to accomodate a large number of students.

Graduation Party

Ahaa! And if nobody ever told you this, the grduation party is both the most important and happiest day and /or event while at Moringa School. It exemplifies the hardwork and long nights that students had to endure during the 6-month period and showcases who stood out among the rest. Every student thinks about this day during their orientaton day, but only a handful manage to see it. Always strive to be among the handful.
